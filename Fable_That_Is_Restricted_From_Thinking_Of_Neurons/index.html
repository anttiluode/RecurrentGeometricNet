Every arm is the same logistic readout on the same signals. Only the feature algebra differs. "Blind" cells are not training failures — they are exact symmetries (test below).

Falsify it

On a clean chirp x and its exact time reversal x′ (circular features): power and depth-1 chiral means are provably identical, F(x′)=F(x); depth-2 means are provably antisymmetric, G(x′)=−G(x); and depth-2 is provably blind to constant pitch. Each chiral layer reads exactly one more derivative of phase: |z| is power, layer 1 is frequency, layer 2 is frequency’s rate of change — the first quantity whose sign flips with the arrow of time.

Why this matters

Any feature built from the power spectrum (equivalently autocorrelation, by Wiener–Khinchin) is invariant under time reversal — so no amount of training lets it tell a process from its rewind. One antisymmetric bilinear product per layer, Im(z·z̄τ), escapes the ceiling, and still reads the arrow at −3 dB SNR (noise louder than signal). Zero hidden units. The same operator is the Chiral Eye’s motion detector; stacking it twice is what hears time.
